Not just pizza.
An Obsession.

We don't believe in shortcuts. Our dough is fermented for 48 hours using wild yeast from Ischia. Our tomatoes grow in the volcanic soil of Mount Vesuvius. Our oven was built brick-by-brick by artisans in Naples.

At Pizza Posto, we honor the tradition of the Pizzaiuolo. Simple ingredients, intense heat, and perfect timing.
